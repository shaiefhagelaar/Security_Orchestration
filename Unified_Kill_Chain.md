# Unified Kill Chain

Paul Pols (Fox-IT / Leiden Univ.) · 2017

An 18-phase meta-framework unifying the Cyber Kill Chain and MITRE ATT&CK. Explicitly models post-compromise activity and non-linear attack progression, covering APTs and ransomware.

3 cycles (18 phases)

    In — Initial access (recon → delivery → exploitation)
    Through — Network propagation & persistence
    Out — Objectives (exfiltration, impact)
